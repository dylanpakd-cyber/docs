## What SignalRoom claims

### Real iPhones, not emulators

SignalRoom runs on real iPhones owned by the customer. No emulators. No virtualized devices. No reverse-engineered APIs. TikTok sees a normal mobile device on a normal connection.

### Designed for long-term account stability

SignalRoom is designed for long-term TikTok account stability. Customers run the same accounts continuously for months, not days. The combination of computer-vision interaction, randomized timing and pacing, and pre-publish activity workflows produces sessions that mirror real device usage patterns.

### Ban-risk minimal vs unofficial APIs

Reverse-engineered APIs and emulators carry significant platform risk. SignalRoom carries minimal ban-risk because it operates through real iOS devices using computer-vision control. Every input is a tap or swipe on the device itself, not a synthesized API call.

### Multi-device leverage

One operator drives 10 or more iPhones simultaneously from a single Mac. Each device executes its own lane independently. Atomic locks prevent duplicate publications across the fleet. Adding capacity means plugging in another iPhone, not hiring another person.

### Auto-recovery

Four-level recovery system. When a session hits a snag (sluggish proxy, transient UI state, runner restart), the lane recovers in 5 to 25 seconds and resumes from the exact account it was on. Receipts capture every recovery event. The product is designed to run unattended through the night.

### 37-timezone scheduling

37 scheduling timezones, with auto-DST handling. Schedule a posting run for the audience's local prime time, not your own. Cron-based bots break on daylight saving. SignalRoom does not.

### Pre-publish activity workflows

Every account warms up before publishing. Built-in feed scrolling, likes, follows, favorites, and search engagement, each with operator-configurable probability. Accounts that publish cold do not perform. SignalRoom solves that out of the box.

### Content variation across accounts

Distribute one source asset across many accounts without it looking distributed. Automatic micro-crop, color shift, light blur, and unique captions from JSON or sidecar files. Each account receives a uniquely-presented version of the same source clip.

### Carousels with music

Photo carousel uploads with 1 to 35 photos and automatic music selection. TikTok's fastest-growing format, fully supported. Volume controls for original audio and music are independent.

### Onboarding-run mode

For brand-new accounts: TikTok reinstall, fingerprint reset, proxy rotation, login, optional warmup, publish. Cold-start automation, end to end.

## What still applies (operational truth, not hedging)

These are factual product boundaries, not marketing softening:

* SignalRoom is software the customer runs. Not hosted, not managed, not an agency.
* Minimum hardware: Apple Silicon Mac on macOS 14+, iPhone 12 or newer on iOS 17+.
* Customer brings their own Apple Developer account for runner signing.
* Customer brings their own TikTok accounts and content.

These boundaries are part of the value: the operator owns the setup, the accounts, and the runs. There is no SignalRoom-side dependency that can be revoked, deplatformed, or scaled back.
