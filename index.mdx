## Safety framing

SignalRoom helps operators make attention workflows visible, paced, logged, and reviewable. It does not guarantee platform or account outcomes. Public docs do not describe hidden, evasive, or detection-avoidance behavior.

If you're paged at this docs site from the SignalRoom landing at [signalrooms.xyz](https://signalrooms.xyz), welcome, the docs are open and you don't need an account to read them.
