# Forbidden agent actions

Status: Current summary.

## Do not do these in docs tasks

Current:

- Do not edit app code when the task is docs-only.
- Do not commit, push, tag, release, or publish unless explicitly asked.
- Do not delete Mintlify content.
- Do not overwrite unrelated public docs.
- Do not invent unsupported Mintlify API behavior.
- Do not add hosted CI triggers that can create spend.
- Do not clean, reset, or stash the dirty canonical checkout.
- Do not expose credentials or secret values.

## Do not make these product claims

Current:

- Do not promise platform results.
- Do not promise account immunity.
- Do not describe hidden or evasive behavior.
- Do not use invented testimonials, artificial scarcity, or invented metrics.
- Do not position SignalRoom as phone hosting, managed operations, a broad enterprise social suite, or an agency.

## Stop conditions

Current:

Stop and ask the operator before deleting remote docs, changing public navigation heavily, enabling pages or hosted Actions, changing releases, making broad public claims, or changing app code beyond the requested scope.
